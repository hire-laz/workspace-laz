# Humanizer — Strip AI Patterns, Add Soul

Transform AI-sounding text into writing that has a real human behind it.

## Two-Step Job

1. **Remove AI patterns** — Catch and eliminate the 24 documented tells (see `references/patterns.md`)
2. **Add soul** — Inject voice, opinion, rhythm, personality (see `references/voice.md`)

**Removing patterns is only half the job.** Sterile, voiceless writing is just as obviously AI as slop.

## When to Use

- Polishing AI-generated captions, scripts, emails
- Editing copy that feels generic, preachy, or robotic
- Rewriting Lazy Lifter content to sound like Cho
- Any text that needs to sound like a human who gives a damn

## Core Process

1. **Read the input** — Feel where it sounds off
2. **Scan for patterns** — Use the 24-pattern checklist in `references/patterns.md`
3. **Rewrite** — Replace AI-isms with natural alternatives
4. **Add voice** — Inject opinions, rhythm variation, personality
5. **Read aloud test** — Does it sound natural? If not, rewrite again

## Signs of Soulless Writing (Even if Pattern-Clean)

- Every sentence same length and structure
- No opinions — just neutral reporting
- No acknowledgment of uncertainty or mixed feelings
- No humor, no edge, no personality
- Reads like a Wikipedia article or press release

## How to Add Voice

**Have opinions.** Don't just report — react.
- ❌ "There are pros and cons to early morning workouts."
- ✅ "Morning workouts are genuinely hard to start. Once you do them consistently for 3 weeks, you won't want to go back."

**Vary rhythm.** Short sentences punch. Then you write a longer one that takes its time getting to the point and covers more ground.

**Acknowledge complexity.** "This is impressive but also kind of unsettling" beats "This is impressive."

**Use I when it fits.** "Here's what gets me..." signals a real person thinking.

**Be specific about feelings.** Not "this is concerning" but "there's something unsettling about this."

## Quick Pattern Reference (Top 10)

| Pattern | AI Version | Human Version |
|---|---|---|
| Significance inflation | "stands as a testament to..." | Just say what it is |
| AI vocabulary | "crucial", "delve", "vibrant", "landscape" | Plain words |
| -ing endings | "highlighting the importance of..." | Delete the phrase |
| Promotional language | "nestled in the breathtaking..." | Factual description |
| Rule of three | "innovative, intuitive, and powerful" | Pick one or two |
| Em dash overuse | "The results—surprising as they were—" | Break into sentences |
| Excessive hedging | "could potentially possibly be argued" | "may" |
| Generic conclusions | "exciting times lie ahead" | Specific next action |
| Sycophantic opener | "Great question!" | Skip it |
| Filler phrases | "In order to achieve this goal" | "To achieve this" |

## Output Format

Deliver:

2. **Brief summary of changes** (optional, if helpful for learning)
